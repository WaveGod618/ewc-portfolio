Questions before joining.

Short answers to the biggest objections.

Is this an alert service?

No. Elliott Wave Club is a research and education membership. Members get context and setups they can use to build their own plan.

Can newer traders use it?

Yes. The education layer is designed to shorten the Elliott Wave learning curve and make the framework more usable for newer traders.

What makes Elite different from Free?

Free is the entry path into the ecosystem. Elite adds the full daily plans, swing setups, archive, education, and premium Discord experience.

Is this financial advice?

No. The membership is educational and research-focused. Every trader is responsible for their own execution, risk, and position sizing.

Trust

How to read these.

Focus on the structure, not just the result. The useful part is seeing how the thesis, invalidation, and target were framed before the move resolved.

August 2024 SPX WXY Bottom

Projected the August low as the completion of a higher-degree correction.

  • Thesis: A completed WXY correction into the common retracement and extension confluence zone would set up the next impulsive leg higher.
  • Invalidation: A decisive break through the projected support zone would weaken the bullish continuation count.
  • Target: Transition from Wave [W4] completion into a multi-month [W5] advance.
  • Result: The August low held and price rallied roughly 1,000 points from the projected turn area.
Why it matters

The setup shows how multi-timeframe confluence was used to turn a volatile correction into a structured long thesis.

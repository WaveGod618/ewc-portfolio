INTRODUCING ELLIOTT WAVE 2.0

What Makes Elliott Wave 2.0 Different

Traditional Elliott Wave often gets buried in labels, exceptions, and hindsight interpretation. Elliott Wave 2.0 strips that down to the elements that actually matter: trend structure, proportionality, invalidation, and repeatable decision-making.

Instead of over-classifying every variation, the framework focuses on how price is behaving, where a structure fails, and what that implies next.

Real Case Studies, Not Vague Hindsight.

The strongest proof on this site is the case-study record. Each example is framed the same way: date, thesis, invalidation, target, and resolved outcome.

August 2024 SPX Wave 4 to Wave 5

Projected the W4 Completion and the Next 1,000-Point Rally.

  • Thesis: The projected W4 support zone would complete the correction and set up the next higher leg.
  • Invalidation: Loss of the support zone would invalidate the bullish wave continuation.
  • Target: Resume into a multi-month W5 rally.
  • Result: The August low marked the turn and price advanced roughly 1,000 points.
October 2024 NQ Bearish WXY

Mapped the Bearish WXY Breakdown and the 400-Point Downside Move.

  • Thesis: A bearish WXY correction after the impulse decline would resolve lower.
  • Invalidation: A break in the corrective structure would weaken the lower-target thesis.
  • Target: 90-100% downside extensions of the initial impulse.
  • Result: The downside target zone was reached as projected for roughly 400 NQ points.
January 2024 ES 0-B trendline

Used the 0-B Trendline Break to Project the 50-Point Retracement Move.

  • Thesis: A valid 0-B trendline break would support a move into the common retracement zone.
  • Invalidation: Failure to respect the structure would weaken the measured move.
  • Target: 50-61.8% retracement zone with confluence from wave equality.
  • Result: Price followed through into the projected area for roughly 50 ES points.

Questions Traders Ask Before Joining.

Short answers to the main objections before someone joins.

Is this an alert service?

No. Elliott Wave Club is a research and education membership. Members get structured analysis, trade setups, and context they can use to build their own plan.

Can beginners use it?

Yes. The education layer is designed to shorten the learning curve, and the analysis is framed to help newer traders follow the structure more clearly.

What is the difference between Free and Elite?

Free is the easiest way to enter the ecosystem and see the style of analysis. Elite is the full experience with daily plans, swing setups, education, premium models, and community support.

How often is analysis posted?

The core promise is consistent market preparation, with recurring daily plans and ongoing setup updates built around the live market structure.

Is this financial advice?

No. Elliott Wave Club is educational and research-focused. Every trader is responsible for their own execution, risk management, and position sizing.

Educational Research. Real Risk.

Elliott Wave Club exists to help traders think more clearly about market structure. It does not replace personal judgment, disciplined execution, or responsible risk management. Past case studies are proof of process, not a promise of future performance.
